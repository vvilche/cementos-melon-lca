Technical Proposal: E-T-A FC01-CA Flowmeter Replacement

The client requires replacing a gaseous flow sensor in a 1" (DN25) line operating under a pressure of **100 mBar**. The original instrument was an **E-T-A FC01-CA** operating on the thermal dispersion/calorimetric principle, which was physically destroyed due to a mechanical impact (personnel fall). The process gas carries **high concentrations of corrosive Chlorine and highly abrasive CKD (Cement Kiln Dust)** at high temperatures.

Process Challenges (Chlorine + CKD + Mechanical Falls)
Gaseous chlorine in the presence of ambient moisture is extremely corrosive to standard stainless steel (316L). The kiln dust (CKD) easily accumulates on hot thermal dispersion probes, acting as thermal insulation and ruining the reading accuracy. Additionally, traditional insertion probes protruding from the pipe are highly vulnerable to physical impacts.

SUPCON Solution Alternatives

We present three industrial solutions from SUPCON specifically engineered to withstand corrosion and CKD abrasion while eliminating any risk of future mechanical breakage:
